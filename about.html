About This Project

This project is a personal initiative aimed at spreading awareness about the growing problem of deforestation in urban areas. With rapid urbanization, trees are often the first to go, making way for concrete. But the cost to the environment and public health is immense.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inform, educate, and encourage action toward preserving and restoring urban green spaces. Trees are essential for a healthy, livable cityâ€”and it is our responsibility to protect them.

Vision

To see a future where urban planning includes the natural environment as a vital part of city infrastructure. A future where cutting a tree is as serious as breaking the law, and where reforestation becomes a standard practice in every city around the world.
